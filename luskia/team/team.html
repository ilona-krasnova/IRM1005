Our team consists of four indivudals who have a passion for saving animals. Ilona, Phoebe, Yasmeen and Luskia dedicate most of their time saving precious animals found in poor environments. The four founded the shelter in 2010 and have accomplished so much in the span of 10 years. In total, they have saved over 250 animals and found loving families for each one of them. The recognized award winning pet shelter, continuously fuflills their mission on a daily basis despite difficult cirmcumstances. The four are always there to save a day and make a difference in these animal's lives. They will provide the saved animal an amazing home, while providing a family a pet that best fits their needs. Choosing the Mamba Pet Shelter will be one the best decisions you will ever make! Our team of professionals promises you that!
