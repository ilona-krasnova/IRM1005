What you need to have before you adopt a dog:

  1. Dog bed
  2. Collar
  3. Leash
  4. Grooming tools
  5. Toys such as stuffed animals, balls, ropes
  6. Food and dishes
  7. Bath supplies (shampoo, conditioner, etc)
  8. Hygiene supplies (toothbrush, toothpaste, nail clippers, etc)

What you need to have before you adopt a cat:

  1. Collar
  2. Litter box + litter
  3. Food and dishes
  4. Grooming tools
  5. Scratching post (so they don't scratch anything else!)
  6. Cat bed (we recomend putting it in a sunny spot!)
  7. Hygiene supplies (toothbrush, toothpaste, nail clippers, etc)

What should I know before I adopt?

  • Make sure that you have enough time to take care of a pet, they need a lot of work. They need attention, to be taken to the vet, fed, groomed, and more.
  • Do you have kids? Do you have any other pets? Take these questions into consideration because every pet is different and will need specific needs.
  • When at home, cats and dogs will need a suitable amount of space to roam around. Be sure that your pet will have enough room and that they won't be too confined. Take the size of the pet into consideration.
  • Do you or friends/family have allergies to cats or dogs?
  • Choose the type and personality of the pet carefuly. You want to have a pet that will bond well with you. For example if you have a lot of energy, the consider a pet that is also energetic.
  • Cats and dogs need to visit the vet quite frequentley. Take into account the cost and time of this.
