Our Mission

Our mission is to find a cat or dog (or both!) that matches you best. Having a pet that matches your personality is so important because it makes you and your pet happy. We also have the goal of rescuing animals that may be stray, lost, or abandoned all around Ottawa. If they are lost, we try our best to return them to the rightful owners. We want to help as many animals as we can because they deserve to have the best care. Finding our cats and dogs loving homes is very important to us.

We hope you enjoy our website!
